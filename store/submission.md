## Description

MicroCiv is a turn-based 4X strategy game built for the Meta Ray-Ban Display — a tiny Civilization that fits in your line of sight.

Found cities, scout the fog, research a 12-tech ladder, race the AI to World Wonders, and conquer rival capitals. Three asymmetric factions (Solaris / Umbra / Tellus), three map sizes, three difficulties, naval combat, ranged units, promotions, Great People, barbarians, and tribal villages — all controlled with the Neural Band: pinch to act, arrows to navigate, double-tap up/down to toggle scroll mode.

No account, no install — open it on the glasses and play.

**Features**
- Turn-based hex 4X — explore, expand, exploit, exterminate
- 3 factions with unique bonuses
- 12-tech tree across 4 ages (Ancient → Modern)
- Ranged combat, fortify bonuses, walls, catapults, siege
- World Wonders, natural wonders, rivers, tribal villages
- Save/Continue between sessions (per-device)
- Designed-for-D-pad UI: no touch, no voice — pure pinch + arrows

**Four ways to win**
- Domination — capture every rival capital
- Science — research all 15 technologies
- Culture — build 4 World Wonders
- Economic — hold 1,500+ gold for 5 turns
