How to Play

Goal: Capture the enemy capital, or research every tech.

Cursor mode (default): arrows move the cursor one tile at a time.

Scroll mode: arrows pan the map. Toggle with ↑ ↓ ↑ ↓.

Zoom: cycle with ← → ← →.

Pinch / Enter: open actions for the tile under the cursor — move units, attack, found cities, manage production, end turn.

Esc: close menus.

End turn from the action menu when nothing is left to do.
